Question 3

(a)

  1. bank
  2. Account
  3. Loan
  4. Customer

(b)

Yes, bank-branch is a weak entity. Its partial key is Branch_no, and its identifying relationship is "Branches" of bank.

(c)

Branch_no tells the number of bank-branch. It is unique in each bank, but it can be the same among banks. Bank-branch is the branch of bank, so there is an identifying relationship.

Question 4

  • Bank is uniquely identified by its code. It also has name and address.
  • Bank-branch is the branch of bank. Every bank has more than one bank-branches and every bank-branch is only one branch of bank. It is uniquely identified by its number in each bank. It also has address.
  • Bank-branch can have more than one accounts and loans.
  • Account is uniquely identified by its number. It also has balance and type.
  • Loan is uniquely identified by its number. It also has amount and type.
  • Customer is uniquely identified by its ssn. It also has name, phone and address.
  • Account and Loan must respectively belong to one bank-branch and more than zero customers.
  • Each customer can have more than zero accounts and loans.
