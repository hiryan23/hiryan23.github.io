具体措施

减少反刍与气体排放

调整日粮结构，改善牛羊等反刍动物好胃口

相关研究发现，通过提高饲喂水平、降低牧草成熟度、降低饲料精粗比，可以使单位肉或奶的甲烷排放量平均减少12%，同时将动物生产力平均提高17%。这主要是通过改善日粮结构，调整日粮营养来调整饲料的适口性，以提升畜禽的采食量和饲料利用率。简单来说，就是让牛羊等反刍动物胃口好，消化好。

常见的操作有秸秆青贮、氨化技术（可使饲料变软），可使单个动物甲烷排放减少16%-30%。此外，多功能舔砖饲喂技术（补充维生物矿物质），如舔盐砖，是以食盐为载体，加入钙、磷、碘、铜、锌、锰、铁等常量微量元素，可以使反刍动物在日增重10%-30%的同时，单位畜产品的甲烷排放量减少10%-40%。

提升饲料质量或添加化学剂

科学管理，抑制或减少产甲烷菌生长

通过补充甲烷抑制剂，饲喂含鞣质的草料、增加电子受体（硝酸盐、硫酸盐），添加脂类、植物提取物等方式可以使甲烷每日排放量平均减少17%。与上述改善牛羊等反刍动物的胃口不同，此举主要是通过抑制产甲烷菌活性，或是不产生甲烷来达到减排的效果。

甲烷抑制剂传统采用的是莫能菌素等抗生素，但是调查研究发现，添加植物提取物，会使瘤胃微生物的抗药性更低，而且在牛羊等反刍动物体内不会造成残留，即减少了甲烷的排放，又保证了肉奶的绿色健康。如海藻、大蒜提取物（英国政府准备给牛羊喂食的一种甲烷抑制剂），多酚植物提取物白藜芦醇（中国科学院研究发现，而且在高精料中抑制效果更加显著）等。

日本北海道大学研究生院的小林泰男教授的研究团队也证明海藻对产甲烷菌的生长有抑制作用。该团队还发现腰果壳的油脂混合到饲料中，可实现甲烷减排20%的效果。此外，该团队开发牛胃传感器，利用AI技术控制牛的喂食。

在新西兰帕默斯顿的北部地区，一种叫做“科布茶”的益生菌抑制剂正被用于减少牛打嗝，也可减少20%的甲烷排放。此外，新西兰的一家企业还专门研发了一款疫苗，可减少30%甚至更多的甲烷排放。而在美国，一款由硝酸和生物酒精制成的粉末产品，已经被40多个国家使用，其甲烷减排效果高达92%。

Production End - From the Perspective of Carbon Emissions (Methodology + Economic Benefits Comparison + Reference)

Ruminal Fermentation

LLONCH et al. [66] summarized the measures for reducing CH4 production in the gut and divided all emission reduction measures into two categories: inhibiting CH4 production and reducing emission intensity. The methods of inhibiting CH4 production include chemical inhibitors, electron acceptors (nitrates), ion carriers (monensin), and dietary lipids. The methods of reducing emission intensity include increasing dietary digestibility, intensive housing, improving health, increasing reproductive efficiency, and optimizing breeding.

Vaccination against methane bacteria

  • Injection of vaccines that can inhibit methane bacteria in the gut
  • Benefit losses are negligible, and methane emissions decrease by 2-5%
  • GONZÁLEZ-RECIO O, LÓPEZ-PAREDES J, OUATAHAR L, et al. Mitigation of greenhouse gases in dairy cattle via genetic selection: 2. Incorporating methane emissions into the breeding goal[J]. Journal of dairy science, 2020, 103(8): 7210-7221.

Breeding and cattle cultivation:

  • Optimize genes and select cattle with less rumination and increased weight
  • Estimated to decrease by 0.5-1% per year using sheep as a reference
  • AgresearchWorld-first research to breed low methane livestock recognised - AgResearch

Manure Management System

Manure treatment and recycling

  • Recycling manure, converting it into biogas, and using it as fertilizer, energy, etc.
  • Reduce methane by 26%
  • Estimation and Reduction of Methane Emissions from Animal Manure (book118.com)

Feeding Crop Cultivation

  • Avoid deforestation for cattle grazing and soybean planting for cattle feed
  • Deforestation for cattle grazing emits around 8GtCO2 per year
  • Data from the Amazon Rainforest

Feed Transportation and Processing

  • Improve feed quality, formulation or add chemicals
    • Straw silage, ammoniation technique (to soften feed) (reduces individual animal methane emissions by 16-30%)
    • Improve diet structure (reduces methane emissions by 12%, increases productivity by an average of 17%)
    • Multi-functional lick brick feeding technology (supplementing vitamins and minerals), such as salt licks (reduces methane emissions per unit of livestock product by 10%-40% while increasing daily weight gain by 10%-30%)
  • Methane-synthesizing feed protein
    • India’s String Bio company
