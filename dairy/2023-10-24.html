要确定美团公司发行的3年期高级别无担保固定利率美元计价债券的息票利率，我们先确定该债券的yield，然后通过yield推算出coupon rate

债券的yield=risk free rate + spread。其中，risk free rate我们取相同期限的美国国债作为标的基准。根据price viewer上面的数据，美国3年期国债（USGB 4.625 10/15/2026，ISIN：US91282CJC64）的yield为4.88%（2023年10月28日数据）

考虑美联储最近的利息波动对美国国债收益率的影响，我们分别采用近1个月的美国3年期国债收益率，进行多项式拟合和移动平均，对确定的无风险利率进行修正。最终考虑各项因素，确定无风险利率为4.87%

对于spread，我们利用price viewer里的filter进行筛选，选出和美团信用评级相同，行业相近的公司，考察其发行的相同种类的三年期高级别无担保固定利率债券的spread。

A-spread（Bid）：相同信用评级，发行种类相同，期限相同。旨在宽泛地判断公司债市场对信用评级的反应程度。

PARAMGL：传媒 KITERE：房地产 AMTOWE：通信 CONABRA：食品消费与包装 NFG：能源

房地产行业杠杆率高，信用利差大。传媒行业无形资产占比高，信用利差较大。这两者所处行业特征与美团差距大，不考虑。剩下四支债券中，CONABRA 7.1250是puttable的，其余是callable的。

取最近1个月的数据进行平均，得到结果。AMTOWE：116.96875 CONABRA：103.89625 CONABRA：87.05125 NFG：116.96875

注意到伴随着巴以冲突等事件，世界经济不确定性增加，整体利差市场有略微上升的趋势。

目前，美团已经发行了两种债券： MEITUAAA 2.125 10/28/2025 Z-spread为108.17（2023年10月28日数据） MEITUAAA 3.05 10/28/2030 Z-spread为249.76（2023年10月28日数据） 光看这两个债券的spread波动率较小，可以看出市场对美团的态度较为稳定。同样作为参考依据。

所以，若发行面值为100USD的3年期高级别无担保固定利率美元计价债券的息票利率可以定为6%

To determine the coupon rate on a 3-year, high-level, unsecured, fixed-rate, U.S. dollar-denominated bond issued by MMT, we first determine the yield of the bond and then derive the coupon rate from the yield.

The yield of the bond = risk free rate + spread, where risk free rate is taken as the benchmark for U.S. Treasuries of the same maturity. According to price viewer's data above, the yield on a 3-year US Treasury bond (USGB 4.625 10/15/2026, ISIN: US91282CJC64) is 4.88% (October 28, 2023 data)

Considering the impact of the Federal Reserve's recent interest rate fluctuations on U.S. Treasury yields, we used U.S. 3-year Treasury yields for the last 1 month, respectively, and performed a polynomial fit and a moving average to correct the determined risk-free rate. The final risk-free rate is determined to be 4.87% considering all factors

For the SPREAD, we utilize the FILTER in the PRICE VIEWER to screen and select firms with the same credit ratings as MMT and in similar industries to examine the SPREAD of the same types of high-level, unsecured, three-year, fixed-rate bonds issued by them.

A-spread (Bid): same credit rating, same type of issuance, same maturity. Designed to broadly determine the extent to which the corporate bond market responds to credit ratings.

PARAMGL: Media KITERE: Real Estate AMTOWE: Communications CONABRA: Food Consumption and Packaging NFG: Energy

The real estate industry has high leverage and large credit spreads. The media industry has a high percentage of intangible assets and large credit spreads. These two are in industries characterized by a large gap with the USG and are not considered. Of the remaining four bonds, CONABRA 7.1250 is puttable and the rest are callable.

Taking the last 1 month's data and averaging it, we get the result.AMTOWE: 116.96875 CONABRA: 103.89625 CONABRA: 87.05125 nfg: 116.96875

Noting the increased uncertainty in the world economy that accompanies events such as the Israeli-Palestinian conflict, the overall carry market is trending slightly higher.

Currently, MEITU has issued two bonds: MEITUAAA 2.125 10/28/2025 Z-spread of 108.17 (October 28, 2023 data) MEITUAAA 3.05 10/28/2030 Z-spread of 249.76 (October 28, 2023 data) Just looking at the smaller spread volatility of these two bonds, you can see that the market is more stable on MEITU. Again, this is used as a reference point.

So, all factors considered, the A-spread of the unweighted ordinary bond of Meituan Corporation is about 113bps, puttable is about 120bps and callable is about 105bps

In summary, the yield of the bond is 6.00% (without weight), the yield of puttable is about 6.07%, and the yield of callable is about 5.92%

Therefore, the coupon rate for a 3-year high-level unsecured fixed-rate USD-denominated bond with a par value of USD100 can be set at 6%
