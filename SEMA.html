Final

Weight: 40% of the course grade.
For the culminating project of this seminar, gather into groups of 3-5 students each. Pick a school of economic thought and a current regional, national, or global economic problem and apply what you have learned in this class to suggest a solution. Give a well-reasoned final presentation (about 5-10 minutes long per group) on how you would use your theory to advise a political or business leader (a mayor, governor, president, or even the UN Secretary-General) on how to fix their problem. Be sure to explain why your chosen problem is significant, walk us through your reasoning on why your solution would help the problem, and defend your plan against the ideas of other economists this course has covered who would propose a different approach. This presentation should be in the form of a speech or slideshow presentation.
