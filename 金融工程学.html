Forward（远期）

定义

A forward contract is an agreement to buy or sell an asset at a certain future time for a certain price.

  • The party that has agreed to buy has what is termed a long position.
  • The party that has agreed to sell has what is termed a short position.

Payoff function

![[金融工程学/dec2b3efcd376ce8845f5c2b5036e39.png]]

细节

  • Forward的价格是谈判的，所以初始payment是0，定价用无套利原则

Option（期权）

定义

An option is the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an asset under specified terms.

  • A call (put) option is an option to buy (sell) a certain asset by a certain date for a certain price.

    • call：认购期权，看涨期权
    • put：认沽期权，看跌期权

  • The price is known as the exercise price or the strike price.

  • The date is known as the expiration date or maturity.

  • An American option can be exercised at any time during its life.

  • A European option can be exercised only at maturity

Payoff function

![[金融工程学/8ad945785a0a4b4811c357915f516a1.png]]

  • The payoff from a long position in options is always nonnegative.（不考虑成本）
  • There is a cost to acquiring an option.

FRA（Forward Rate Agreement）

保证在\(T_1\)到\(T_2\)时刻，本金\(L\)能享受到\(R_K\)（一次复利等价）利率。至于啥时候保证，在\(T_1\)时刻之前都行。

价值

FRA价值是相对于远期时的市场利率\(R_M\)的价值。但是我们不知道\(R_M\)，只能用远期利率\(R_F\)来表示。不同时刻FRA价值不一样，所以FRA价值的确定和时刻（该时刻的远期利率）有关。

0时刻价值

FRA收到现金流 \[V_{FRA}=L(R_K-R_F)(T_2-T_1)e^{-R_2T_2}\]

FRA借出现金流 \[V_{FRA}=L(R_F-R_K)(T_2-T_1)e^{-R_2T_2}\]

也可以用现金流折现法来推导：

0 1 2
\(+L(R_K-R_M)(T_2-T_1)\)
\(\Leftrightarrow\) \(+L(1+R_K)(T_2-T_1)-L(1+R_M)(T_2-T_1)\)
\(\Leftrightarrow\) \(-L\) \(+L(1+R_K)(T_2-T_1)\)

所以价值为： \[-Le^{R_1T_1}+L(1+R_K)(T_2-T_1)^{R_2T_2}\]

套期保值（期货对冲）

本质是用期货锁定价格。在未来多空某商品，在现在多空期货来进行套期保值。

基差风险

理论上，完全对冲需要满足的条件： - 存在资产对应的期货 - 时间对的上

实际上，很难完全对冲： - 没有对应品种的期货 - 没有对应时间的期货

理论计算

假设\(t_1\)开仓，\(t_2\)平仓 ![[金融工程学/a518c690d5f79dd81015a3051a761b3.png]]

Long Hedge: \(R=-F_1+F_2-S_2=-F_1-b_2\) Short Hedge: \(R=F_1-F_2+S_2 = F_1+b_2\)

总结：对买进行对冲，结果要花\(F_1+b_2\)的钱；对卖进行对冲，结果收到\(F_1+b_2\)的钱

交叉对冲

用与商品价格相关度高的期货对冲 \[\Delta S =a+b\times \Delta F + \varepsilon\]

对冲比例\(h\): \[h=b=\cfrac{\text{Cov}(\Delta S,\Delta F)}{\text{Var}(\Delta F)}=\rho\cfrac{\sigma_S}{\sigma_F}\]

本质原理：降低未来价格的不确定性

降低方差

有效价格\(S+\Delta S-h\Delta F\)

\[\begin{align} \min_h \text{Var}[S+\Delta S-h\Delta F]\\ \Rightarrow h^*=\cfrac{\text{Cov}(\Delta S,\Delta F)}{\text{Var}(\Delta F)}=\rho\cfrac{\sigma_S}{\sigma_F} \end{align}\]

具体结果

\[N^*=h^*\cfrac{Q_A}{Q_F}\] - \(Q_A\): size of position being hedged (units) - \(Q_F\): size of one futures contracts (units)

对冲误差分析

  • 取整时存在误差
  • 计算参数（相关系数、方差等）取自历史数据，存在估计误差
  • 每天结算制度导致误差

例子：用股指期货对冲权益资产

思路

我已经持有了权益资产，因此我想要在未来以一个价格卖出权益资产，因此我要锁定价格，用short index futures的方法

原理

根据CAPM模型， \[R_S=\alpha +\beta\times R_F+\varepsilon\]where \(R_S=\cfrac{\Delta S}{S}\) and \(R_F=\cfrac{\Delta F}{F}\)

所以\[h^*=\beta\cfrac{S}{F}\] \[N^*=h^*\cfrac{Q_A}{Q_F}=\beta\cfrac{S\times Q_A}{F\times Q_F}=\beta\cfrac{V_A}{V_F}\]
